# Daily Executive Brief Template
## Automated 7:00 AM MDT Delivery

### Brief Structure

#### **🔥 HIGH VOLTAGE (Immediate Action Required)**
```
Template:
- [ITEM]: [Brief description]
- **Action Required:** [Specific action Brett needs to take]
- **Deadline:** [When action is needed]
- **Context:** [Why this is urgent]

Example:
- School Contact: Ashton attendance issue reported
- **Action Required:** Call Brighton High (801-826-5800)
- **Deadline:** Today
- **Context:** Escalating pattern detected, intervention needed
```

#### **📊 Family & Personal Updates**
```
Template:
- **Academic:** [Grade updates, assignment notifications, school events]
- **Health:** [Medical appointments, billing notifications, service reminders]
- **Activities:** [Music programs, sports, family events]
- **Positive News:** [Achievements, milestones, family moments to celebrate]

Example:
- **Academic:** Jaxton geography assignments completed, Ashton photography course progress
- **Health:** Cameron Wellness receipt ready for review
- **Activities:** BHS Drumline preparation for family night
- **Positive News:** Family attendance at school events shows strong engagement
```

#### **💼 Business Intelligence**
```
Template:
- **LTK Updates:** [Website analytics, business communications, strategic developments]
- **Industry Intelligence:** [Competitive insights, market trends, opportunity identification]
- **Action Items:** [Business tasks derived from email analysis]

Example:
- **LTK Updates:** Google Search Console monitoring active for lendertoolkit.com
- **Industry Intelligence:** Ski industry renewal deadlines coordinated (IKON, Brighton, Deer Valley)
- **Action Items:** Consider seasonal marketing timing insights for LTK campaigns
```

#### **📅 Calendar & Deadlines**
```
Template:
- **Today:** [Immediate deadlines, appointments, events]
- **This Week:** [Important upcoming items]
- **Upcoming:** [Future items to keep in mind]

Example:
- **Today:** IKON Pass renewal deadline (April 16)
- **This Week:** Brighton loyalty rates expire April 20
- **Upcoming:** Summer IT internship applications (deadline approaching)
```

#### **🌤️ Contextual Information**
```
Template:
- **Weather:** [Relevant for outdoor activities, travel, or family planning]
- **Ski Conditions:** [During season - snow reports, resort status]
- **Transportation:** [Traffic, travel conditions if relevant]

Example:
- **Weather:** Clear skies, 68°F - Good day for outdoor family activities
- **Ski Conditions:** Season ended, but parking passes available for next season
- **Transportation:** Standard conditions, no travel impacts expected
```

### **Brief Delivery Protocol**

#### **Standard Delivery (7:00 AM MDT):**
```
Channel: Discord #bess
Format: Structured text with clear sections
Tone: Executive summary - concise, actionable, professional but warm
Length: Target 5-7 bullets total, readable in 2-3 minutes
```

#### **High Voltage Escalation:**
```
If HIGH VOLTAGE items present:
- Lead with urgent items
- Include specific action steps
- Highlight deadlines clearly
- Provide context for urgency
```

#### **No Critical Items:**
```
If no urgent items:
- Focus on positive family/business updates
- Highlight opportunities (deadlines, events)
- Include relevant contextual information
- Maintain daily connection without creating false urgency
```

### **Brief Generation Process**

#### **Data Sources:**
1. **GBrain Query:** Recent High Voltage patterns, family updates
2. **Email Analysis:** Last 24-48 hours of critical communications
3. **Calendar Integration:** Today's schedule, upcoming deadlines
4. **Pattern Recognition:** Ongoing situations (Ashton attendance, business projects)
5. **Weather/Context:** Relevant environmental factors

#### **Quality Standards:**
- **Actionable:** Every item should enable a decision or action
- **Concise:** No information overload, focus on what matters
- **Prioritized:** Most important items first
- **Contextual:** Include enough background for informed decisions
- **Personal:** Maintain family and business context balance

#### **Backup Procedures:**
- **System Failure:** Manual brief generation within 30 minutes
- **No Critical Items:** Positive update brief (family/business highlights)
- **Brett Unavailable:** Archive brief for later review
- **Technology Issues:** SMS backup delivery if Discord fails

### **Brief Customization Rules**

#### **Business Priority Scenarios:**
- **LTK Critical Days:** Business intelligence takes priority
- **Industry Events:** Relevant market intelligence highlighted
- **Strategic Planning:** Competitive insights emphasized

#### **Seasonal Adjustments:**
- **Ski Season:** Snow conditions, resort updates, pass renewals
