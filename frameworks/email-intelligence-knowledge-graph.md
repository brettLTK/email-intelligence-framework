# Email Intelligence Knowledge Graph Framework

## Concept: Marketing Emails → Domain Intelligence

Transform inbox spam into strategic intelligence through relationship mapping and trend analysis.

```

├── INTERESTS
```

### Intelligence Extraction Methods

#### From Marketing Emails, Extract:
1. **Industry Trend Signals**
   - Product category evolution
   - Pricing trend changes
   - Seasonal campaign patterns
   - Technology adoption cycles

2. **Behavioral Targeting Analysis**
   - How companies segment users like Brett
   - Cross-industry targeting patterns
   - Demographic assumptions in messaging
   - Purchase prediction algorithms

3. **Competitive Intelligence**
   - Market positioning strategies
   - Feature prioritization trends
   - Brand messaging evolution
   - Partnership/collaboration patterns

### Implementation Strategy

1. **Email Parsing** → Extract structured data (sender, category, timing, content themes)
2. **Relationship Mapping** → Connect emails to Brett's interest/behavior graph
3. **Trend Detection** → Identify patterns across time and categories
4. **Intelligence Synthesis** → Generate insights from aggregated patterns
5. **Dynamic Filtering** → Present relevant views based on context/priority

### Business Application (LTK)
This framework scales to:
- Executive inbox intelligence for strategic decision-making
- Industry trend monitoring for competitive advantage
- Customer behavior analysis from communication patterns
- Predictive modeling for business opportunity identification

## Key Insight
"Spam" becomes intelligence when properly categorized and analyzed. Every marketing email contains behavioral assumptions about the recipient - reverse-engineering those assumptions provides competitive intelligence about market segmentation and targeting strategies.
