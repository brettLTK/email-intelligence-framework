# EPIC Kickstarter Detection Framework

## Definition: What Makes a Kickstarter "EPIC" for Brett?

### Brett Identity Alignment Signals
- **Outdoor/Adventure Tech:** Camping, hiking, skiing gear innovations
- **EDC/Tools:** High-quality, durable, multi-functional tools
- **Tech Innovation:** Genuinely breakthrough technology (not incremental gadgets)
- **Craftsmanship:** Artisan-made, premium materials (titanium, carbon fiber)
- **Problem-Solving:** Solves actual problems Brett faces

### EPIC Criteria (All Must Apply)
1. **Innovation Score:** Genuinely new approach, not just marketing variation
2. **Quality Indicators:** Premium materials, proven manufacturing
3. **Practical Application:** Solves real problem in Brett's lifestyle
4. **Funding Success:** 500%+ funded (proves market validation)
5. **Creator Credibility:** Track record of successful product delivery

### AUTO-REJECT Patterns (Hide from View)
- Generic gadgets with flashy marketing
- Incremental improvements on existing products  
- Cheap materials (plastic, basic aluminum)
- First-time creators with no track record
- Products unrelated to Brett's interests/lifestyle

### Telemetry Data Collection
From Kickstarter spam, extract:
- **Industry Trends:** What's being marketed to outdoor/tech enthusiasts
- **Price Points:** Market pricing for various product categories
- **Innovation Patterns:** Common "breakthrough" claims vs actual innovation
- **Behavioral Targeting:** How companies segment and target users like Brett

## Implementation
- **High Signal:** EPIC items surface to Brett for consideration
- **Medium Signal:** Industry intelligence for trend analysis
- **Low Signal:** Hidden from view but data captured for profiling
- **Spam Intelligence:** Use volume/patterns for inbox owner profiling research

## Business Intelligence Value
Marketing emails become research data:
- Consumer behavior targeting strategies
- Industry trend identification
- Pricing and positioning intelligence
- Product category evolution tracking
